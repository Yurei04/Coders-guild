About Coders Guild

×

Joining the Coder Guild is straightforward and open to all students within the CITCS department. Here’s how you can become a member:

Ask the CITCS Department or Professors: Reach out to your CITCS department office or speak directly with your professors who are involved with the guild. They can provide you with information about joining and any upcoming events or meetings.

Contact the Organizers: Look for announcements or posters around the campus, especially within the CITCS buildings, that list the organizers' contact information. You can directly approach them to express your interest in joining.

Scan the QR Code: There are QR codes displayed on bulletin boards and around the CITCS department that you can scan with your phone. This will direct you to an online form or provide you with more details on how to join the guild.
